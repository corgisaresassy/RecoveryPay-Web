Paying Your Bill

RecoveryPay connects addiction treatment facilities and their patients. We mail statements on behalf of addiction treatment centers to patients. If you have questions about your statement, please call your provider directly.

How To Pay

RecoveryPay accepts all major credit and debit cards. Paying is as simple as clicking on the pay now button at the top of the page. We will automatically forward the payment to your facility and ensure your payment is reconciled. Please allow up to two weeks for processing. You may receive additional statements during the processing period.

Once you've paid your bill, you will receive a final statement in the mail confirming your payment and that your balance has been cleared. To the extent you have questions or concerns on your bill, please reach out directly to your treatment provider. Their information is included at the top of your statement should you have questions.

Understanding Your Bill

Your statement was generated using the information provided by your treatment provider. Within the statement you will find:

  • The name, address and phone number of your rendering provider

  • The services provided by your rendering provider

  • The amounts paid by your insurance company for these services

  • The amounts remaining which you owe, these are typically composed of your deductible, co-pay, or co-insurance

Why Do I Have To Pay?

As part of your insurance coverage, you are responsible for paying for services until your deductible is met, paying any co-pays associated with your service and any co-insurance amounts. Without these your insurance company would not typically offer coverage at all.

Our goal is to help your treatment provider contact you about these amounts and receive payment as required by law. If you are unable to pay today, please contact your facility directly to discuss a payment plan.

What is RecoveryPay?

RecoveryPay and You

RecoveryPay works on behalf of your treatment provider to help ensure you receive your statement and understand what it entails. We work to ensure your sobriety team is able to continue treating patients like you in the future!

Once you've completed treatment and your insurance company has paid their portion of your bill, your treatment center is required by law to seek payment for your portion. RecoveryPay helps treatment centers connect with patients and ensure legal compliance and receive payment.

If you have any questions about your bill or why you are receving one, please contact the facility listed on your statement for more details.

What Does This Statement Mean?

Your insurance company has indicated that you are personally responsible for some amount of the total bill. The statement you received lists the services you received and the amount you owe. Please pay today to prevent further action.

How Do I Pay?

Paying is easy with RecoveryPay! You can click on the Pay Now link in the header to securely make a payment on your bill. We will work with your treatment provider to then close your account and mail you a closing statement. Once you receive a closing statement, your obligation has been met.

What If I Can't Pay Today?

If you believe there's a mistake on your bill, the amount you owe, or need more time to pay, we suggest you reach out directly to the treatment center listed on your billing statement to discuss your bill or a payment plan.
