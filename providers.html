Our Collections System

Finding a way to maintain compliance and collect on patient responsibilities can be challenging in any environment. RecoveryPay takes care of this entire process for you. From statements to collections, we have you covered!

Integration

RecoveryPay integrates directly into your EMR and automatically pulls patient responsibility and relevant data for you based on a customizable set of options. We then automatically generate and issue statements to patients with simple and fast payment options.

Statements

RecoveryPay automatically generates, prints and mails statements based on your preferences, unique language and facility logo and style. These statements and follow up collection letters ensure patients clearly understand their responsibility and can pay quickly and easily.

Simple, Low Pricing

RecoveryPay only charges 10% of collections plus postage and credit card fees. We are typically anywhere from 200%-400% cheaper than the competition thanks to our proprietary automation and industry experience. Contact us today to see about getting started!

What Do Patients See?

You control every aspect of the statements and associated language issued to your patient. We do not conduct high pressure collections calls or issue threatening emails. Our simple system respects the patient while maintaining compliance and collecting effectively.

How Do I Get Paid?

RecoveryPay passes through funds every month to ensure we can complete accurate posting before disbursement and process any necessary refunds or overpayments. Our team handles all reconciliation in house to make the process as easy as possible on you.

How Do I Get Started?

RecoveryPay doesn't charge any set up fees or ongoing base fees. Getting started is as simple as signing the agreement, getting us access to your EMR and watching the payments roll in.
